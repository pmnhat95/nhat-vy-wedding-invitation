How He Proposed

We decide to live together, never apart, joined as one heart.

He proposed on a Scrabble board.

He glued the letters on, spelling "LOISE WILL YOU MARRY ME", and then asked if I wanted to play the game after dinner. He had me set up the game, and of course when I opened the board, I saw the letters spelling out the proposal!

I was so awestruck, confused and surprised at the same time as he stood there ring in hand. I framed the board and have it hanging in our home.

"Yes! Yes of course! Yes!"
